Umlambo Foundation (www.umlambo.org)

 

 

Umlambo Foundation is a non-profit organization that contributes to the national effort of improving the quality of education in South Africa' schools.
Umlambo Foundation uses the goodwill of people and institutions to invest in the leadership and management of school principals, in order to improve the quality of education in public schools – giving learners a chance to win against poverty.
