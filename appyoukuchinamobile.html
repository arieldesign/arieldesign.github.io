China Mobile Limited  is a Chinese state-owned telecommunication company that provides mobile voice and multimedia services through its nationwide mobile telecommunications network. The company is one of the largest mobile telecommunications companies by market capitalization today. As of July 2013, China Mobile is the world's largest mobile phone operator by subscribers with about 740 million.
